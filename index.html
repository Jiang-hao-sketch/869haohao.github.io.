Proposed Architecture

1 is broken down into modality-specific sensory encoders, an observation trunk, and an action head predicting a chunk of actions. 1 takes as input observations from multiple camera views, robot proprioceptive state and a task instruction and enables performing closed-loop control at 10Hz in our real world experiments on the xArm.
